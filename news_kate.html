Good Gets at the Auction

We had a blast at the Boston Blossom Auction! Jenny finally pulled out her diamond-studded wallet and actually forked over a few bills for a new set of rare ghost orchids. Thanks, Jenny! It only took 3 years! Now that we have managed to acquire more than a few of the extra-special wild orchids that (we are pretty sure) are super illegal to possess, since they could only be taken from a wild and protected environment by black market plant poachers, we will finally be able to crush those losers from Jacksonville in the next East Coast Anther Convention! Get your ikebana bands on, and prepare to destroy!!
